Goal Setting & Time Estimates

Breaking big goals into "Micro-Wins" prevents the feeling of paralysis.

Big Goal Micro-Win (The Task) Est. Time
Structure a Form Define a <fieldset> to group related inputs. 15 mins
Build Navigation Create a <ul> with five anchor tags. 15 mins

Key Takeaways

  • Break it down: Split complex concepts into bitesized lessons to build confidence.
  • Use Visuals: Flowcharts and color-coding help organize program logic.
  • Rest and Reward: Incorporate breaks to stay refreshed and celebrate every tiny milestone.
